Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making GK Library accessible to everyone.

Our Commitment

GK Library is committed to providing a website that is easy to use and accessible for all visitors, including people with disabilities. We continually work to improve the user experience for everyone.

Accessibility Features

  • Simple and easy-to-read website layout.
  • Clear navigation menus.
  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
  • Readable fonts and good color contrast.
  • Images include descriptive alternative (alt) text where appropriate.
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation for important pages.
